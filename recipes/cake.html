Cake

Description

Cake is a food and a block that can be eaten by the player.

A cake with candle is a light-producing variation created when a candle is used on an uneaten cake.

Ingredients

  • 3 buckets of milk: Obtained by using empty buckets on cows
  • 2 sugar: Obtained from sugarcane
  • 1 egg: Obtained from chickens
  • 3 wheat: Obtained through farming

Steps

  1. Obtain ingredients as described above, and sort them alphabetically on a kitchen counter.
  2. Layer noodles, cheeses, and sauce in a oven safe pan until you experience a pan overflow.
  3. Continue doing this until you reach the stack pointer.
  4. Upon reaching the stack pointer, add a sticky note that says "RETURN COOKED LASAGNA" to the top of the lasagna.
  5. If all has gone well, you will have cooked lasagna on your table, otherwise, evacuate a 1 mile radius around the lasagna immediately.
