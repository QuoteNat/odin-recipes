Lasagna

Description

This page is not actually going to be an accurate list of steps to make lasagna. I apologize to any who have come to this page looking for real lasagna instructions.

Follow the subsequent instructions on this page at your own peril.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Preferably from a deer
  • Onion and garlic: These can be obtained from ogres and vampire hunters respectively
  • Tomato products: Go to your local are farmer's market, or optionally start your own tomato farm
  • Sugar, Spice, and everything nice Seasonings: While you can buy these ingredient individually, It is more cost effective to obtain them from one of the many "build a child from scratch" kits available on Amazon
  • Lasagna Noodles: These can be made by hitting spaghetti with a hammer repeatedly
  • Cheeses: Walk to Wisconsin for these
  • Egg: Get a chicken and wait

Steps

  1. Obtain ingredients as described above, and sort them alphabetically on a kitchen counter.
  2. Layer noodles, cheeses, and sauce in a oven safe pan until you experience a pan overflow.
  3. Continue doing this until you reach the stack pointer.
  4. Upon reaching the stack pointer, add a sticky note that says "RETURN COOKED LASAGNA" to the top of the lasagna.
  5. If all has gone well, you will have cooked lasagna on your table, otherwise, evacuate a 1 mile radius around the lasagna immediately.
